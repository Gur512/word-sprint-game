    Welcome to Word Sprint!

    Test your typing speed and accuracy in this fast-paced word game! Here's how to play:

    • Click the Start button to begin. You will see random words appear on the screen.
    • Type each word exactly as it appears in the input box below. Matched words will disappear, and you'll earn points!
    • You can’t delete or edit matched words—so type carefully!
    • Compete with yourself and others by securing a spot on the scoreboard. The top 10 scores will be displayed!

    Good luck, and let the typing sprint begin!
